My Favourite Movies

Here are the list of some of my favaourite movies.

  1. A silent Voice

    • Release Date: September 17, 2016
    • Director: Naoko Yamada
    • Genre: Animation, Drama, Romance

    A heart wrenching and emotionally powerful story about a deaf girl and her relationship with a boy who bullies her. What I love about this movie is how it explores themes of redemption, healing, and the power of human connection.Dealing with themes of bullying, disability, and redemption, the movie offers a poignant and thought-provoking narrative that resonates with audiences of all ages.

  2. Princess Mononoke

    • Release Date: July 12, 1997
    • Director: Hayao Miyazaki
    • Genre: Animation, Adventure, Fantasy

    A visually stunning and thematically rich film that explores the conflict between nature and human civilization. The movie is a masterpiece of storytelling, with complex characters and a thought-provoking narrative that delves into themes of environmentalism, spirituality, and the consequences of human actions on the natural world.
